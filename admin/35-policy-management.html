1. Purpose

This policy outlines the guidelines for employees who wish to work from a location other than the company's offices.

2. Eligibility

  • Employees must have completed their probation period.
  • Roles must be compatible with remote work.
  • Performance must be satisfactory (Rating > 3.5).

3. Requirements

Employees must have a stable internet connection and a secure workspace. All company data policies apply regardless of location.
